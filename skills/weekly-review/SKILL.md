# Weekly Review

Keep research moving forward with structured weekly reflection.

## Review Protocol

1. Read `references/review-template.md` for the full template
2. Check `research/interests.md` for direction alignment
3. Check `research/connections.md` and `research/ideas.md` for updates
4. Identify the ONE most important thing for next week

## Four Questions

Answer these each week:

1. **What did I do?** — Papers read, experiments run, ideas had
2. **What was the biggest insight?** — One sentence max
3. **What am I stuck on?** — Specific blockers
4. **What's the ONE most important thing next week?** — Not 5 things, 1 thing

- [ ] Direction priorities aligned with `interests.md`?
- [ ] Any new connections found?
